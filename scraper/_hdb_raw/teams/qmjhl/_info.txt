<h2 class="team_header">QMJHL History</h2>
<img style="float:right; border-style:thin; border-color:black; margin:10px; " src="/ihdb/logos/ll/tn_qmjhl_2019-20.gif" alt="2019-2020 QMJHL logo" />
<img style="float:right; border-style:thin; border-color:black; margin:10px; " src="/ihdb/logos/ll/tn_qmjhl_1997.gif" alt="1996-1997 QMJHL logo" />
<img style="float:right; border-style:thin; border-color:black; margin:10px; " src="/ihdb/logos/ll/tn_qmjhl_1977-78.gif" alt="1977-1978 QMJHL logo" />
<img style="float:right; border-style:thin; border-color:black; margin:10px; " src="/ihdb/logos/ll/tn_qmjhl_1974-75.gif" alt="1974-1975 QMJHL logo" />
<div style="padding:10px;">The Quebec Major Junior Hockey League is a Major Junior hockey league that has operated from 1969 to the present. The QMJHL was founded via a merger of the Quebec Metropolitan Junior Hockey League and the Quebec [Provincial] Junior A Hockey League. The league was known as the QJAHL for its first two seasons until renaming itself its present name.</div>
